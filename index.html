We are a team of University of Michigan students with a passion for horses.


WHO WE ARE

From beginner Walk-Trot to riders with years of experience in the top equitation classes, the Michigan Equestrian Team believes every rider has something to offer and learn from riding with us. We compete through the Intercollegiate Horse Show Association(IHSA) in Zone 6, Region 4. Those not interested in competing or have no riding experience are also welcome to join the team and ride with us!

In addition to the competitive aspect of riding, the team offers social events, which provide a great opportunity to meet new people and make friends. Social events range from movie nights to cookie decorating, as well as a end-of-year banquet. Other activities include fundraising events and volunteering at a local horse rescue, Starry Skies Equine Rescue and Sanctuary.

PRACTICE


Team practices are held every other week. Flat riders practice Tuesday evenings and Jump riders practice Wednesday evenings. Practices are held at Willowbrooke Farm in Plymouth, MI. Lessons are available in addition to practice; information is available on Willowbrooke's website. (Any showing member who wishes to jump is required by IHSA to take lessons weekly.)


SHOWING

The Michigan Equestrian Team is part of Zone 6 Region 4 of IHSA. We compete against schools in Michigan and in Canada. Generally, there are between five to eight shows per year, including a double-header that our team hosts. Entries in each show are determined by our coach based on ability, participation in practice and lessons, as well as other team events. Riders earn individual points based on what they place in their class. Additionally, one rider per division is able to win points for the team. Riders who acquire 36 points have the opportunity to compete at Regionals. Those who place first or second at Regionals will move on to Zones, and from there, potentially move on to Nationals

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS



Do I need my own horse?

Absolutely not! In fact, most team members do not own horses or do not have their horses at college with them. In IHSA, the host college provides all of the horses for the shows, so there is no need to own your own horse.

Do I need to have any riding experience?

No, there are devisions for every level of rider, so we need riders from all experience levels!

Where do you ride?

Our team is pased at Willowbrooke Farm in Plymouth Michigan, which is owned and operated by our coach, Jennifer Blades.

Are there tryouts?

We do hold evaluation riders to give our coach, Jen, a chance to see you ride and ensure you are placed in the correct division. We do not cut anyone from the team.

Do I need a car?

No, we all carpool to practice, lessons, and shows, so there is no need to own a car.

Do I need my own equipment?

You do not need to own any tack. Any member who wants to show must have the correct show attire. However, if you do not own show clothes, we can almost always find clothes that fit you to borrow from other showing members.
